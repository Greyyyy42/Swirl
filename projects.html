My first project: Harmony bridge

My first school project; harmony bridge is a project I made with the help of my team. our goal was to make a swing bridge with s truss system that can score a low deflection magnitude by only using palm tree parts, as a prototype to solve the problem of urban congestion in Damietta Branch. The mechanism of the bridge is functioning by a servo motor controlled with an Arduino UNO and an ultrasonic sensor.

My second project: Zenith Vortex

my second project; Zenith Vortex, is a Hybrid VAWT that combines both Darriues and Savonuis VAWT to generate electricity, the project involves storing electricity from the turbine using a generator in batteries, and also giving a certain power output. This project was made by me and my new team this semester.
