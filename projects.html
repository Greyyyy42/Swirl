My School Capstone Projects

Two remarkable projects from my STEM journey

Harmony Bridge

My first team project was a swing bridge with a truss system designed to minimize deflection using only palm tree parts. This prototype aimed to solve urban congestion in Damietta Branch, featuring a servo motor controlled by an Arduino UNO and ultrasonic sensor.

Zenith Vortex

A hybrid vertical-axis wind turbine combining Darrieus and Savonius designs to generate electricity. This project included energy storage in batteries and power output measurement, developed with my new team this semester.
